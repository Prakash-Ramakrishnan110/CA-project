Advantages of a Limited Liability Partnership

Why LLP is the preferred choice for professionals and growing businesses

Limited Liability Protection

Partners are not personally liable for business debts - liability is limited to their contribution.

Flexible Management

Operate like a partnership while enjoying corporate benefits - no board meetings required.

Tax Advantages

No dividend distribution tax - Profits taxed at partner level only (avoid double taxation).

Easy Compliance

Fewer regulatory requirements compared to private limited companies.

Credibility

'LLP' suffix enhances professional reputation with clients and vendors.

Easy Transferability

Ownership can be transferred by changing partnership agreement.
