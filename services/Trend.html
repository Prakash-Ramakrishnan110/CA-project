India's Transfer Pricing Landscape

Regulatory Evolution

Since 2001, India's transfer pricing regulations have expanded to cover both cross-border and domestic transactions, with Section 92BA specifically addressing specified domestic transactions.

Compliance Framework

The CBDT mandates detailed documentation including Master File, Local File, and Country-by-Country Reporting, with increased scrutiny on intra-group services and royalty payments.
