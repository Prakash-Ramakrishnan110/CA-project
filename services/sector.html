FDI-Prohibited Sectors

Understanding the sectors where Foreign Direct Investment is restricted or prohibited in accordance with government regulations and policies.

National Security

Sectors critical to national defense and security are completely off-limits to foreign investment.

FDI-Prohibited Sectors Overview

Complete Prohibitions

  • Lottery business including government/private lottery
  • Gambling and betting including casinos
  • Chit fund and Nidhi company activities
  • Atomic energy generation

Restricted Sectors

  • Defense (up to 74% with approval)
  • Telecom (up to 100% with approval)
  • Private security agencies (up to 74%)
  • Civil aviation (up to 100% with conditions)
These restrictions are subject to change based on government policies.

National Security Sectors

The following sectors are completely prohibited from foreign direct investment due to national security concerns:

  • Defense equipment manufacturing (beyond 74%)
  • Cryptography and security technologies
  • Critical infrastructure protection
  • Border area development projects
These restrictions are strictly enforced to protect national interests.

Overview

Key sectors with FDI restrictions and their governing regulations

Defense Sector

Prohibited beyond 74%

Foreign investment in defense industries is capped at 74% under automatic route and requires government approval beyond 49%.

Key Regulations:
  • FDI Policy 2020, Para 5.2.18
  • Arms Rules 2016
  • Defense Production Policy 2018

Atomic Energy

100% Prohibited

All activities related to atomic energy generation and related technologies are completely prohibited for foreign investment.

Key Regulations:
  • Atomic Energy Act 1962
  • FDI Policy 2020, Para 5.2.4
  • Complete prohibition under Schedule I

Space Technology

Restricted

FDI in space sector is permitted up to 100% in specific activities with government approval, while certain areas remain prohibited.

Key Regulations:
  • Space FDI Policy 2020
  • Satellite Communication Policy
  • IN-SPACe Guidelines

Lottery & Gambling

100% Prohibited

All activities including government/private lottery, gambling, betting and casinos are completely prohibited for foreign investment.

Key Regulations:
  • FDI Policy 2020, Para 5.2.4.4
  • Public Gambling Act 1867
  • State-specific lottery regulations
