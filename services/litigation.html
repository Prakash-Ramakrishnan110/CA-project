Understanding Litigation

Litigation refers to the process of taking legal action through the court system to resolve disputes. It involves a series of steps from pleadings to trial, where parties present evidence and arguments before a judge or jury.

  • Formal dispute resolution process through courts
  • Governed by procedural rules and evidence laws
  • Can involve individuals, businesses, or government entities
  • May result in judgments, injunctions, or settlements
