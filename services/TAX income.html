Key Rules & Regulations for Expatriates

U.S. Tax Requirements

  • Filing Requirements: Worldwide income reporting regardless of residence
  • Form 2555: Foreign Earned Income Exclusion ($120,000 for 2023)
  • FBAR (FinCEN 114): Required for foreign accounts exceeding $10,000
  • FATCA: Form 8938 for specified foreign assets

International Considerations

  • Tax Treaties: Over 60 U.S. treaties to prevent double taxation
  • Residency Rules: Vary by country (183-day rule common)
  • CFC Rules: Controlled Foreign Corporation regulations
  • Local Compliance: Host country payroll/tax requirements

Important Compliance Notice

Penalties for non-compliance can be severe, including fines up to $10,000 per violation for FBAR and 40% of underpaid tax for FATCA violations. Many expatriates qualify for streamlined procedures if they need to correct past filings.
