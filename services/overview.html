Overview

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) plays a crucial role in the economic development of India. The government has established a comprehensive regulatory framework to facilitate foreign investments while ensuring compliance with national priorities. The approval process varies depending on the sector, investment amount, and whether the investment falls under the automatic route or government approval route.

Our firm specializes in navigating these complex regulations to ensure seamless approval for your foreign investment proposals.
