Key Benefits of Private Limited Company

Why entrepreneurs and businesses choose Private Limited as their preferred business structure in India

Limited Liability Protection

Shareholders' personal assets are protected. Liability is limited to their share capital contribution.

Business Credibility

Enhances trust among customers, vendors and investors compared to proprietorship firms.

Separate Legal Entity

Can own property, incur debts, and sue/be sued in its own name, distinct from its owners.

Easy Fundraising

Ability to raise capital through equity funding from investors and venture capitalists.

Tax Advantages

Lower corporate tax rates and various deductions available under Income Tax Act.

Perpetual Succession

Continues to exist regardless of changes in ownership or management.
