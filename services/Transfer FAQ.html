What is your service turnaround time?

Standard documentation takes 7-10 business days. Complex cases may require 2-3 weeks. We offer express services (3-5 days) for urgent needs.

Do you handle advance pricing agreements (APAs)?

Yes, we specialize in unilateral, bilateral, and multilateral APAs. Our team has successfully negotiated APAs with the Indian CBDT and foreign tax authorities.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have deep expertise in:

How do you ensure compliance with BEPS?

Our methodology aligns with OECD BEPS Action Plans, particularly Action 8-10 (intangibles) and Action 13 (documentation). We incorporate country-by-country reporting requirements and master file standards.

What's included in your audit support?

Our end-to-end audit defense includes:

  • Response drafting
  • Comparable analysis refresh
  • Representation before tax authorities
  • Dispute resolution strategies
