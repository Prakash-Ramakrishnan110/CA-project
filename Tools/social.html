Frequently Asked Questions

In India, employers must comply with several social security schemes including:

  • Employees' Provident Fund (EPF) - Mandatory for organizations with 20+ employees
  • Employees' State Insurance (ESI) - Applicable to establishments with 10+ employees
  • Gratuity - Payable after 5 years of continuous service
  • Professional Tax - Varies by state

We help clients navigate these requirements while optimizing their contribution structures.

Expatriate social security requires careful analysis of:

  • Home country social security obligations
  • Host country requirements
  • Applicable totalization agreements between countries
  • Duration of assignment

We develop customized solutions that consider all these factors to ensure compliance while avoiding unnecessary costs.

Penalties vary by jurisdiction but can include:

  • Monetary fines (often calculated per employee per day of non-compliance)
  • Interest charges on overdue contributions
  • Legal proceedings against company directors
  • In extreme cases, restrictions on business operations

Our compliance reviews help identify and rectify potential issues before they result in penalties.
