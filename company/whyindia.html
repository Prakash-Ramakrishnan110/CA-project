Key Investment Advantages

India offers unique benefits that make it an attractive destination for global investors

Rapid Economic Growth

India is the world's fastest-growing major economy with GDP growth consistently above 6%. Projected to become the 3rd largest economy by 2030.

Large Consumer Market

1.4 billion population with a growing middle class. Young demographic with 65% of population below 35 years driving consumption.

Skilled Workforce

World's largest pool of STEM graduates. Highly skilled, English-speaking professionals available at competitive costs.

Business-Friendly Reforms

Simplified tax regime (GST), improved ease of doing business rankings, and liberalized FDI policies across sectors.

Infrastructure Development

$1.4 trillion National Infrastructure Pipeline. World-class digital infrastructure with 750+ million internet users.

Strategic Location

Gateway to South Asia and ASEAN markets. Favorable time zone for global business operations across US, Europe and Asia.

Government Initiatives

Pro-business policies and programs to support investors

Make in India

Flagship program to transform India into a global manufacturing hub. 100% FDI allowed in most sectors with simplified processes.

  • 25+ sectors covered including automobiles, aviation, and electronics
  • Production Linked Incentive (PLI) schemes worth $26B
  • Single window clearance for approvals

FDI Policy Reforms

One of the most liberal FDI regimes globally with automatic approval routes for most sectors.

  • 100% FDI allowed in most sectors under automatic route
  • Tax incentives for R&D and infrastructure investments
  • Special economic zones with tax holidays
  • Improved intellectual property rights protection
